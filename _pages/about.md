I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at Clemson University, where I focus on enhancing the security and resilience of autonomous perception systems. My work bridges the fields of computer vision, machine learning, and cybersecurity, addressing critical challenges in the development of trustworthy autonomous technologies.

Autonomous perception systems are at the core of innovations like self-driving vehicles, drones, and intelligent robotics. My research explores vulnerabilities in these systems, aiming to develop robust solutions that ensure their safe and reliable operation in dynamic and adversarial environments.

Through a combination of theoretical insights and practical implementations, I contribute to advancing the state of the art in autonomous system security while fostering interdisciplinary collaboration. Feel free to explore my website for more about my research, publications, and professional activities.
